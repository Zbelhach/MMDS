Aim and scope

Big data is a growing interdisciplinary field of research with a high impact in sciences and technologies. It opens new perspectives and brings numerous challenging problems, in particular in mathematical modeling and computer science. The aim of the conference is to highlight such challenging problems and recent developpements in the areas of mathematics, models for data science. It also brings together researchers and experts from different fields involving high level modeling and large scale computing.
The conference is adressed to a large public of academics, engineers and indutrials.
The inscription is free but mandatory.
